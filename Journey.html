Beautiful Art And Music

The game's soundtrack was composed by Austin Wintory. The composer worked closely with the developers, particularly sound designer Steve Johnson and programmers, to create music that would change depending on the user's actions and the game environment. The soundtrack was subsequently nominated for a Grammy Award.

The release of Journey attracted over 100+ industry awards and media accolades, with some naming the game as their ‘Game of The Year’ in 2012.
